## Guardrails

The LLM Guardrail module provides a set of safety mechanisms to ensure that the outputs of large language models are aligned with ethical guidelines and organizational policies. It includes features such as:
- **Content Filtering**: Automatically detects and filters out inappropriate or harmful content.
- **Bias Detection**: Identifies and mitigates biases in the model's responses.
- **Policy Compliance**: Ensures that the model's outputs comply with predefined organizational policies.

For more details, refer to the [Guardrail Documentation](link-to-documentation).
